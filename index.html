Pre-race

  • 💚 Remember to bring your green Cisco shirt - so it's easy to spot you on exchange
  • 💚 Meet at the stairs in fornt of the Majorstuen metro station, towards Kirkeveien
  • 💚 Get your start number from the team lead (first runner)
  • 💚 Say hi to the persons you will be exchanging with
  • 💚 Take team photo
  • 💚 Head over to your stage in good time before your expected start time

The teams

The name of the team doesn't match the order in which the teams start or the pace - it's just the number given by the organizer.

The race
